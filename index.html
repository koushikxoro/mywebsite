My Journey

Frankly, speaking you will get very few candidates who have changed school every now and then, I am one of those very few candidates who have changed school frequently it is not that I had some problem… LOL…. but my family condition made me to do so as, I have studied in all types of school from government to high class private schools but in the end what I learned is that it’s student like us who makes the school not the other way round. I have done my 10th standard from Assembly of God church school, Ukhra in 2013 and there I secured 91%, after that I moved to DAV Model school Durgapur for science stream but due to my family problems I suffered from mental depression, and failed to achieve what I aimed for i.e., IIT I thought that every thing has ended in my life so I have taken a gap of 1 year for preparing for IIT but due to sheer pressure I failed again and went for engineering in a state government college….for information sake I have scored 87.67 % in 12th.

Regarding my college life I have lots to say, I entered college as if I am a failure being a student from general category I have already faced lots of “struggle”…LOL… . I didn’t have any idea how my engineering life would be firstly I thought that if I only focus on my curriculum I will win the race, but it turned out to be very different, I learnt making projects doing programming is the first thing that you should do in your daily life first year passed too swiftly and I scored 7.98 YGPA, in my second year I have made a plan to explore the culture of engineering so I went to different IIT colleges fest and learnt there culture but somehow latter on I realised that it is just waste of time, in second year I have scored a YGPA of 8.54. Third year was relatively easy as I had clear goals ahead of me, I had started doing projects, competitive coding etc, but again time flied away too swiftly by hanging out with friends and I have scored a YGPA of 8.79, 4th year was mostly based on my placement preparation, the first offer that I got was from Infosys as a system engineer role which I later upgraded it to a higher position, I had also different offers from IBM and TCS and in 4th year my YGPA was 9.33, so my DGPA stands at 8.69.
